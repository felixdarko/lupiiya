We are a Community

We understand that entrepreneurship can be alienating and isolating from society, especially during the startup phase of business. So we want to be there with you on your journey to success. So consider us an unpaid team member in your start up.

Technology you can use.

Our technology is designed to ease the financing process for businesses. While the technology is sophisticated and cutting edge, it is not complicated to use. Our tools do not require an MBA or a background in investing. They are easy to understand and use effectively no matter your background.

Legitimise your side hustle.

We know that every one of us in Uganda has a side hustle. But did you know that with some structure and capital, your side hustle could become your main job? Did you know that your side hustle can employ dozens of your peers and contribute significantly to Uganda’s economy? That is the opportunity we are offering.

Investing the Ugandan way.

We Ugandans have our own way of financing our local projects and events. We rely on our community of Friends & Family to finance social events from wedding receptions to graduation parties. We are offering you the chance to use this already tried and tested method to finance your business, and to have your friends and family members as legitimate investors in your company.
