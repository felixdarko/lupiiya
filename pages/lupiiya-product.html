Introducing

Equity crowdfunding made for Uganda.

Lupiiya Hub is our custom built equity crowd funding platform, designed specifically for the needs of Ugandan entrepreneurs and investors. Lupiiya Hub comes with its own set of unique features, built for the African market that ease the financing process for both investors and entrepreneurs.

Accounting Made Simple

Lupiiya Books is an accounting application that was designed to ease book keeping and accounting for SMEs and youth owned startups. One of the main blockades to accessing funding for many Ugandan entrepreneurs is a lack of diligent book keeping and accounting. Recognizing this, we set out to build an accounting tool to help Ugandan small businesses and entrepreneurs stay on top of their accounts and in so doing, increase their chances for financing.

How it Works

A member of the Lupiiya Community can simply download the mobile application, open an account and start to use it. It is easy to use, you simply have to enter your daily incomes and expenses, along with any relevant information and the application will generate ready to use and share statements of accounts, reports and expected taxes. Lupiiya Books also has a savings tracker that enables users to save and plan for their financial futures.

  • Lupiiya Books enables the entrepreneur to account for their business’ financial dealings and operations. It helps you keep track of your expenses, revenues, budgets, debtors, creditors and much more.
  • Lupiiya Books is very easy to use even for an entrepreneur without a background in accounting or finance.
  • Lupiiya Books is free to use for every entrepreneur and investor in the community so don’t miss out on this chance to get professional accounting for free.
  • It also makes it easier for entrepreneurs to pay their taxes and comply with the government’s laws and regulations governing businesses.
  • Lupiiya Books also makes it easy for entrepreneurs to share their financial statements with any and all interested parties, provided they have accounts on Lupiiya Books. But even if they don’t, the statements can be exported and shared on other media.
